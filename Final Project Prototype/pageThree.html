Jurassic Period

There were many huge creatures that roamed the Jurassic Period. However, there are none that can compare to the famous Brachiosaurus.

If you're familar with Jurassic predators, then you'll recognize the Ceratosaurus. A unique predator for its time, it had a horn that was either used for display, as a weapon, or possibly to attract mates.

With plates on its back and a spiked tail, Stegosaurus was a sight to behold! When provoked, Stegosaurus would flush blood into its plates to give predators a warning; leave or face a devastating blow.

Prior to the Cretaceous period, flying pterosaurs were beginning to become more widespread in the Jurassic Period. Dimorphodon was one of them.
